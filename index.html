Understanding the trade-off

Shorter term (e.g. 15-20 years)

Higher monthly payments, but you pay much less interest overall and own your home sooner. Could save tens of thousands of pounds.

Longer term (e.g. 30-35 years)

Lower monthly payments making it more affordable day-to-day, but you pay significantly more interest over the life of the loan.

Quick example: £240,000 loan at 4.5%
